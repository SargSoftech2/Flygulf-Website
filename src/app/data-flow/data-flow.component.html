2024 VERIFICATION PROTOCOL

DataFlow Process

The complete document lifecycle for international healthcare licensing.

01

Passport

Front & back high-res scans. Must have 6 months validity.

02

Degree / Diploma

University certificates verified via Primary Source Verification.

03

Council Registration

Active RNRM or Medical License from your home country.

04

Experience Certs

Validated clinical service history from previous employers.

05

Good Standing

Verification of professional conduct from your local council.
