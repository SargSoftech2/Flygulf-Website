What is BLS Course?
Basic Life Support (BLS)

📅 Feb 20, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read 🏥 Health & Medicine

Basic Life Support (BLS) is an American Heart Association course which is now made mandatory for most of the health care professionals and other employees.

This course prepares to act promptly and effectively in an episode of Cardiac Arrest, Respiratory Arrest, and Choking. This course is intended for health care providers involved in direct patient care either in or out of the hospital. Duration of the BLS course is ONE DAY from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm.

Upon successful completion of the course, participants are given course completion cards which are globally valid for 2 years.
